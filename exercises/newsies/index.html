Breaking News: Rob Learned A Thing

Salt Lake City, UT -- Against all odds, it seems that Rob DeGeorge -- a current V School student -- has actually learned something. After lots of banging his head against the table and claiming he would use his very expensive laptop as a very effective tool for creating a hole in the window, Rob has reportedly learned some basic HTML and CSS code. According to an anonymous source, he may have even learned some super basic Javascript. This report has yet to be confirmed, but shows that he may actually be able to survive the coming months after all.
